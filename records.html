Records!

Why?

One my favorite hobbies besides listening to the radio, is it to collect old records on different places and digitalize them to preserve their history. No one has documented most of records made and published in Chile so my goal is to preserve some of this history. I have neved uploaded this. This website will be full of this information as I have time to add it.

What are them?

I have a bunch on records that I inherited years ago and I've been buying from a while. Most of them are 7" 45 RPM records that are commonly sold just for $1 dollar.

My favorite records are made in Chile because they are high-quality and pretty cheap compared to US and European editions. The main differences are that some titles are translated to spanish and de label is not made of cardboard, they are made on a photograph over a plastic label. Weird, but it works pretty well.
